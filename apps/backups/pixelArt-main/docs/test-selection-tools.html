ðŸš€ Usage Instructions

Selection Tools:

  1. Click the mouse pointer icon to open the Selection Tools menu
  2. Choose Rectangular, Circular, or Lasso selection
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to make your selection
  4. Release to finalize - selected pixels will be copied to buffer
  5. Use Ctrl+C to copy to clipboard (notification will show)

Lighten/Darken Tool:

  1. Click the adjust icon (sun/moon) or press K
  2. Enter a brightness factor when prompted
  3. 1.2 = lighten, 0.8 = darken, 1.0 = no change
  4. The entire active layer will be adjusted
